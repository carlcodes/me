SpartaHack

Spring 2015

During my senior year of college, I was apart of a student-run organization to create Michigan State's first hackathon. My role on the committee was building the Android Application to be used by the attendees.

App features:

  • Schedule of hackathon
  • Push notifications to the attendees for schedule updates
  • Campus map, for the non-students who attended.
  • A contact page, which webhooked into an organizer slack channel.

300 hackers attended and ~$25k given out in prizes.
